Iced London Fog

Descriprion

This iced London fog is a creamy Earl Grey tea iced latte with a hint of vanilla,
as refreshing on a hot day as a cool mist.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup water
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla syrup, such as Torani
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  • Step 1

    Bring water to a boil; remove from heat and add tea bag. Steep for 5 minutes.

  • Step 2

    Fill a tall glass with ice; pour tea over ice. Pour in milk.
